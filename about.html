About Us

Empowering Youth & Communities Through Sports, Education, and Culture

Our Origin

From a Sports Field to a Global Movement

MASA World Foundation began in 2013 with a simple observation: a playground is the best classroom. What started as a small initiative to organize local sports leagues for underprivileged youth quickly revealed a deeper need.

Over the years, our mission expanded. We integrated education to sharpen minds and cultural programs to strengthen roots. Today, MASA is not just an NGO; it is a movement that bridges the gap between grassroots potential and global opportunity.

Our Mission

“To empower youth and communities by providing access to sports, education, and cultural opportunities that foster discipline, leadership, self-reliance, and social responsibility.”

Our Vision

“To build a world where every young individual has the opportunity, guidance, and confidence to lead positive change—locally and globally.”
